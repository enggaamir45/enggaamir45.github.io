About Aamir

I am a front end and back end web developer, a graduate of Electronics and Communication Engineering with huge passion of creating interactive and user friendly websites for small businesses. I keep in touch with latest technologies and strive to produce websites which are easy to maintain and are extensively user friendly in design.

After graduating from Electronics and Communication Engineering branch in 2012, I worked in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia as an Electronic Engineer. I started building web pages as a part time hobby and later decided to make a career in which I have so much passion and interest. Before making a personal websites of my own, I have gone through each and every concept of web design and now I feel like I'm ready to feel the heat and work for real projects.

I have expertise in front end development on:
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Javascript

and back end development on:
  • PHP
  • MySQL

I also have some design and photo editing knowledge on Adobe Photoshop.

I have made many projects at home to enhance my coding skills and currently I am looking for some new projects to work with. I am looking to work as a freelancer and help small businesses with my web design skills.

Currently, I am working in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia and very open to work on any project which comes in front of me. I am always available to discuss about any project on whatsapp, skype, email or direct phone contact.

If you want to know what I do and what I create, see my portfolio. Feel free to contact me and follow me on social media if you are interested to work with me or want to discuss any project.

Follow me to see my latest design and development projects and stay in touch to discuss for any freelance projects.
